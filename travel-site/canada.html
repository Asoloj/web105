Canada

I've always wanted to visit Canada. Just a vacation or a fishing trip with some friends would be awesome. The nature out there looks so incredible, it is one of the places in the world I'd love to visit the most.

If I got to visit Canada I would probably rent a cabin for the weekend somewhere, and go fishing and just explore everything, including the famous Niagra Falls.

A little about Canada

Canada is the second-largest country in the world, a massive part of North America. The Capital city is Ottawa, and the largest city is Toronto. Canada's population is about 37 million. The name Canada is accepted as coming from the St. Lawrence Iroquoian word kanata, which means village or settlement.
