In Mexico, sunflowers are thought to soothe and heal chest pain.

 

Sunflower oil also has an anti-inflammatory effect.

Sunflowers can also help reduce cholesterol

 

Sunflowers are capable of self-pollinating.

Unfortunately, there are a few things sunflowers are in danger of.

 

Fusarium, is a filamentous fungus that is found largely in soil and plants. It is a pathogen that has caused an increasing amount of damage and loss of sunflower crops, some as extensive as 80 percent of damaged crops.

 

Downy Mildew is another disease to which sunflowers are susceptible. Sunflowers'susceptibility to downy mildew is very high due to the sunflower's way of growth and development. Sunflower seeds are generally planted only an inch deep in the ground. When such shallow planting is done in moist and soaked earth or soil, it increases the chances of diseases such as downy mildew.

 

Another threat to sunflowers is broomrape, a parasite that attacks the root of the sunflower and causes extensive damage to sunflower crops
