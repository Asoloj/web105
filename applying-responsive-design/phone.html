For this project all my placeholder images are going to be sunflowers! Because why not?

The real name for a Sunflower is actually Helianthus annuus.

The color of sunflowers can range anywhere from yellow, orange, red and anywhere in between.

As the name suggests, sunflowers grow the best under the full sun.

Some say sunflowers get their name because they follow the sun, however this isn't really true.

For the most part, sunflowers stay fixed in an east-facing direction.
