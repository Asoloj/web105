The fruit of the sunflower, also know as the seeds, can be used in a variety of ways

Sunflower seeds can be eaten raw or roasted, and with or without salt.

Sometimes sunflowers can also be processed into sunflower butter.

Sunflower seeds are also used in bird seed, can be ground up into flour, or used in salads

The sunflower happends to be the state flower of Kansas, as well as the National flower for Ukraine.

Sunflowers are native to the Americas, and there are about 70 species of sunflower.

Each sunflower is actually thousands of tiny flowers grouped together.

Fun fact, sunflowers should be harvested in the morning instead of the morning.

Sunflowers can grow very tall. The tallest sunflower recorded was 30 feet tall!
