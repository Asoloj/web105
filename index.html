Call of Duty: Modern Warfare Review

Overview

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare is a first-person shooter video game that is developed by Infinity Ward and published by Activison The game was released on August 23rd, 2019.

The game is the sixteenth installment in the Call of Duty franchise. Modern Warfare features a story campaign, multiplayer, and the now massively popular battle-royale, Warzone. It is only the second game after Call of Duty: Ghosts to not include a zombies game mode.

Modern Warfare-Campaign

The game's campaign is played through two different characters, switching back and forth between the perspectives of CIA operative Alex, and SAS Sergeant Kyle Garrick. Some missions from the game include recovering toxic gas from Russians, and dealing with the terrorist clan Al-Qatala.
